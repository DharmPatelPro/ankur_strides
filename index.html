About Us

Strides.inc, based in India, operates across four critical sectors: Pharmaceuticals, Chemicals, Energy, and Food. With a commitment to innovation and sustainability, we strive to enhance the quality of life worldwide.

Mission Statement: To provide quality-driven solutions that enrich lives and create a sustainable future.

Vision Statement: To lead with integrity and innovation, adding value across industries for global betterment.

Core Values: Quality, Innovation, Integrity, and Sustainability.
